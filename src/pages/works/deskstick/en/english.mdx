## Features
### Sync your stickies with iCloud.
You can sync your stickies with iCloud. You can use it on multiple devices.
### Organize your notes.
It has an organize window, that you can choose notes which you would like to display. You can also display this window from menubar.
### Always front
Note can be pinned. Pinned note is always displayed in front even if you select another app window.
### Colorful and creative.
You can set the color of the note, and it will also be synchronized via iCloud.

## Use cases for the AI era — keep your prompts and LLM outputs handy

DeskStick works particularly well as a lightweight companion for anyone who uses generative AI (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.) every day.

### A snippet board for prompts you reuse
Templates such as "code review prompt", "meeting notes summarizer", or "translate to English" can each live on their own sticky. They stay reachable while you bounce between browser tabs and chat apps, ready to be copied into the next AI conversation. Color coding makes it easy to separate work, personal, and experimental prompts at a glance.

### A draft pad for Slack, email, and chat messages
Before you hit send on something written in the heat of the moment, write it on a sticky and read it back — that one extra beat is what saves you from "I shouldn't have said that". Drafting on a sticky instead of directly in the recipient's input field also avoids accidental sends and misfires. Especially valuable for sensitive customer replies, reports to your manager, and anything you'll regret sending unedited.

### An always-pinned scratchpad for fleeting thoughts
A pinned sticky stays on top of every other app, which makes it the perfect always-on scratchpad for whatever pops into your head — meeting notes, a URL or number you want to keep nearby, a to-do you'll think about later. Having a "park it here for now" surface always visible in the corner of your screen lets you offload thoughts without breaking flow.

### A scratchpad for LLM outputs
When an AI returns a code snippet or a draft, you often want to compare a few versions before committing them to your editor or document. DeskStick acts as a **disposable scratchpad** for those LLM outputs — pin a sticky on top so it stays visible no matter which app is in focus.

### Cross-Mac AI workflows
Start drafting a prompt on your desk Mac and finish it on your MacBook on the go. Because notes sync via iCloud, the constant "save a fragment, pick it up somewhere else" rhythm of working with AI fits naturally with DeskStick.

### Compared to Apple's built-in Stickies
macOS ships with a "Stickies" app, but DeskStick adds **iCloud sync** and **menubar quick access**, both of which make it well-suited to high-frequency note taking around AI tools.

## How to use
### Select action from the Menubar
  You can select "Show All Stickies" and "New Sticky" from the menubar.

![menubar](/stickies-menubar.png)

### The buttons of the sticky note

| Button | Function |
|--------|----------|
| X | Close the sticky note |
| Pin icon | Keep the sticky note always on top |
| ^ | Minimize the sticky note |
| Trash can icon | Delete the sticky note |
| Palette icon | Change the color of the sticky note |
| Binder icon | Copy the content of the sticky note |
